About Our Foundation

Inspired by our grandmother's legacy of education and empowerment

Tribute to Janakiyamma

The Janakiyamma Foundation is proudly named after our cherished grandmother, a deeply loving soul who has nurtured our entire family with boundless care.

Over the years, she has witnessed remarkable transformations in the world, from humble beginnings to the technological marvels of today, guiding our family through each era with her warm heart and gentle wisdom. Her profound appreciation for the value of education has been a constant beacon, reflecting her belief in its power to shape lives.

In her name, we are inspired to extend this legacy, especially as we see artificial intelligence (AI) reshaping the world at an unprecedented pace. To prepare the next generation to embrace this rapid change, we have established this foundation to provide free, high-quality AI and machine learning education to children and adults worldwide.

Our Story

The Beginning

Starting with a simple vision to provide AI/ML education to kids and adults who are still largely unaware of the latest changes happening in our digital world, the Janakiyamma Foundation was born from a desire to democratize access to these life transforming skills.

Global Vision

While we start small with family connections, our vision extends globally. We believe that quality AI/ML education should be accessible to learners everywhere, regardless of their economic background.
